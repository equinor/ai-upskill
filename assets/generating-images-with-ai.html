Generating images with AI

Some AI systems can generate images, but there are risks involved. Poor quality images, biased content, and fake information are common. Understand the risks and think twice before sharing.

✓ Do...

Read the Equinor image guidelines
apps.equinor.com/photos
There are similar guidelines for infographics and other visual content.
Use the mediabank
equinor.fotoware.cloud
You will find many thousands of photographs, illustrations, and icons.
Label generated content
apps.equinor.com/gl0896
For full transparency, provide the prompt and the model in a caption.

✗ Don't...

Use personal or sensitive information in the prompt
Know the privacy policies of the systems you use.
Generate images of real people
Prefer photos of real situations.
Generate images of Equinor's business activities or facilities
Stick to real photos or use illustrations.
Generate logos or other brand elements
Use design services.
